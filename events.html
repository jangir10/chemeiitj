Events organised by the ChE department:

The Department of Chemical Engineering organizes a range of events throughout the year, including seminars, workshops, and conferences, aimed at fostering academic exchange, promoting research and innovation, and engaging with industry and society.

CHEM-E-SORPTION

Chem-E-Sorption is a unique amalgamation of intriguing competitions, invited lectures from academia and industry, panel discussions and other activities.

INDUSTRY VISITS

Industrial visit is a part of an engineering course, during which students visit industry and get insights on the internal working environment of it.

CHEMCON

It bringS together researchers, academicians, and industry professionals to share their latest research findings and innovations in the field of chemical engineering.

HYDROGEN VALLEY

Conference organised by the department to see the future of hydrogen in Jodhpur and discuss use of solar energy for green hydrogen production.
